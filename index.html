Revelation 13:16-17 (KJV): Economic control - cannot buy or sell without the mark

What it represents:
  • Allegiance to human religious-political authority
  • Accepting changed law (Sunday worship) over God's command
  • Choosing convenience, economy, survival over obedience to God

God's Seal

Revelation 7:2-3 (KJV) "And I saw another angel ascending from the east, having the seal of the living God: and he cried with a loud voice to the four angels, to whom it was given to hurt the earth and the sea, Saying, Hurt not the earth, neither the sea, nor the trees, till we have sealed the servants of our God in their foreheads."
Revelation 14:1 (KJV) "And I looked, and, lo, a Lamb stood on the mount Sion, and with him an hundred forty and four thousand, having his Father's name written in their foreheads."
God's Seal represents:
  • Loyalty to God alone
  • Keeping ALL of God's commandments, including the unchanged Sabbath
  • Having God's name/character written in the mind and heart

The Choice

Revelation 14:9-12 (KJV) "And the third angel followed them, saying with a loud voice, If any man worship the beast and his image, and receive his mark in his forehead, or in his hand, The same shall drink of the wine of the wrath of God, which is poured out without mixture into the cup of his indignation... Here is the patience of the saints: here are they that keep the commandments of God, and the faith of Jesus."

The Final Appeal

Revelation 18:4 (KJV) "And I heard another voice from heaven, saying, Come out of her, my people, that ye be not partakers of her sins, and that ye receive not of her plagues."
Revelation 14:7 (KJV) "Saying with a loud voice, Fear God, and give glory to him; for the hour of his judgment is come: and worship him that made heaven, and earth, and the sea, and the fountains of waters."

Notice this echoes the Sabbath commandment (Exodus 20:11 KJV): "For in six days the LORD made heaven and earth, the sea, and all that in them is, and rested the seventh day."

The Core Issue:
The mark of the beast isn't about technology or physical marks. It's about WORSHIP and OBEDIENCE.

The Question: Will you follow human tradition and changed laws, or will you remain faithful to God's unchanged commandments?
This is not about hating any group of people. Many sincere, loving Christians are in various churches and don't yet understand these prophecies. God is calling all honest hearts to study His Word and follow truth wherever it leads.
